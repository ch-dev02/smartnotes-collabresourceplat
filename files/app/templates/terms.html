Terms and Conditions

Welcome to SmartNotes, a collaborative revision platform where users can submit notes and URLs to other students. These resources will have their keywords extracted to summarise their content. Users can leave ratings on resources to help other students too.

Information Collection and Use

By signing up for SmartNotes, you agree to provide us with your email address. You can also upload PDFs, TXT, and VTT files, and type notes and URLs. We will use this information to provide the services on our platform, and to improve the user experience. Your information will be shared amongst other users who are in your class, but it will be left anonymous. Only the website creator and members of staff from the university can see the information.
